About Me

In 2017, I moved to Austin, Texas in search for inspiration. Inspiration never came, but I did stumble upon an advertisement for a coding bootcamp in town. I signed up for their Web Design track, and in 2018 I was introduced to the beautiful world of coding. I'm still learning, but my favorite part of the web design process is working with clients and helping them build their dreams into a digital reality. Since then I've moved back to Honolulu, Hawaii where I am currently based. Below is a visual portfolio of current and past clients.
