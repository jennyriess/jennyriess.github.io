About Me

In 2017, I moved to Austin, Texas in search of creative inspiration. While there, I stumbled upon an advertisement for a coding bootcamp in town. I signed up for their Web Design track, and in 2018 I was introduced to the beautiful world of coding. I'm still in the learning process, but my favorite part of the web design process is working with clients and helping them build their dreams into a digital reality. Since then I've moved back to Honolulu, Hawaii where I am currently based. Below is a visual portfolio of current and past clients.

{Happy} Clients

This was a relatively easy project, and the collaboration was seamless. J + J Hikes

An online blog created on the Wordpress platform.

J+J Hikes was a collaboration with artist and friend Joel Gaspar to share our compilation of hiking photos taken during 2014-16. This was my first time building a site through Wordpress, and though it's a strong platform, eventually our site would be bombarded my advertisements and restricted on customizations without purchasing a premium account, so I decided to test other platforms. By learning new Content Management Systems (Wordpress, Squarespace, Shopify, and a Wix site I'd rather forget...), I can empathize with clients struggles to find the right fit, and would like to use my own experience to make this process easier with less #%$@! moments. Thus begins my journey as a freelance web developer and designer.

To visit this site, please check out www.jjhikes.com
